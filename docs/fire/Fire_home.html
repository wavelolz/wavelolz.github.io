Fire Ex.


Fire Ex. is a rock band come from Kaohsiung, founded in 2000.They've got famous when Sunflower Student
Movement took place in 2014. At that time, the leader of student asked Fire Ex. to write a song to
encourage the student who took part in this movement. Eventually, the lead singer in Fire Ex. gave
out a song named "Island Skylight", which made Fire Ex. became such a huge hit in Taiwan instantly.
